Travel can be categorized by trip purpose and more. Each travel type offers unique opportunities and challenges, and understanding the purpose of your trip can help you make the most out of your travel experience:

  • Relaxation travel: a travel type that focuses on rest, rejuvenation, and self-care, offering a break from the stresses of everyday life.
  • Ecotourism: a sustainable travel type that focuses on responsible exploration of natural environments while minimizing impacts on the environment and promoting conservation efforts.
  • Adventure travel offers thrill-seekers an opportunity to explore the great outdoors and immerse themselves in new cultures while participating in physically challenging activities.
